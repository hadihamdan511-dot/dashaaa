CHAPTER TEN ✦ FOR YOU

A Letter

My love,

I still remember the night I first texted you. November 15, 2025. I don't know what made me do it — maybe it was courage, maybe it was something I can't name. But I'm grateful every single day that I did.

The first kiss, just after midnight on November 16th. I didn't sleep that night. I don't think I needed to.

December 13th — I asked you to be mine. And when you said yes, something inside me settled. Like I'd been searching for something without knowing it, and then suddenly — there you were.

Seven months. Seven months of late nights and early mornings. Of walks that felt like movie scenes. Of cuddling and laughing and being completely, honestly ourselves with each other. I didn't know it could feel this easy. I didn't know love could feel like home.

And then I left. I watched Belarus disappear below the clouds and I felt it — that weight in your chest when you leave someone who has become necessary to your life. You watched my flight. I know you did. And I carried that with me all the way to Lebanon.

The distance is hard. I won't pretend otherwise. But my love — distance is just a chapter. It is not our story. Our story is still being written, and it is beautiful.

Today you turn 18. And I am in Lebanon, wishing I could be there. But I made you this — every photo, every video, every word — because you deserve to feel how loved you are. Even across 3,000 kilometers. Even across time zones. Even from the other side of the world.

You are my home. You have been since November 15th. And I'm not going anywhere.

Happy birthday, my love. 💗

— Yours, always ❤️
